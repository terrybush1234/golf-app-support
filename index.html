Privacy Policy for GolfApp

Last Updated: July 29, 2024

Thank you for using GolfApp ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle your information. By using our application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personally Identifiable Information (PII)

  • Administrator Account: When a league administrator creates an account, we collect your email address and password via Firebase Authentication to secure your account and verify your identity.
  • Member Information: League administrators enter information about league members, which includes their name, handicap, and optionally, their email address. This is used for core app functions like creating tee sheets and sending league communications.

b) Usage Data

  • We use Google Analytics for Firebase to collect anonymous information about how the App is accessed and used. This may include device type, operating system, and in-app interactions (like which tabs are viewed). This data is aggregated to help us improve the App.

c) App and Game Data

  • We store all data required for the app's functionality, including league settings, schedules, timezones, player signups, and game scores. This data is stored securely in Google Firestore.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the core functionality of the App.
  • To provide administrator functions and account management.
  • To send automated emails for league events based on the schedules you configure.
  • To gather analysis and valuable information so that we can improve our App.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

  • Google Firebase: All app data is stored on Google's secure infrastructure. You can view Google's Privacy Policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Nodemailer (via Gmail): Our server uses Nodemailer to send automated emails. These emails are processed by Google's mail servers.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Core Data: Your account information and league settings are retained as long as your league is active.
  • Transient Game Data: To keep the database efficient, game-related data (signups, scores, etc.) older than 7 days is automatically deleted every Sunday morning.
  • Account Deletion: You may request the deletion of your account and all associated data by following the instructions at our data deletion page.

5. Security

We use industry-standard services (Google Firebase) to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
